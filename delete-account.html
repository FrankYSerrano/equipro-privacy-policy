Request Account Deletion

Users of EquiPro may request deletion of their account and associated personal information.

To request account deletion, please send an email to:

equiproapp@gmail.com

Subject:
Account Deletion Request

Please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Your team or club name (if applicable)

Once we verify your request, your account and associated personal information will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except for information we are legally required to retain.

If you have any questions, please contact us at equiproapp@gmail.com.
